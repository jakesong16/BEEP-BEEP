Driving Detector

Beep Beep AI is a machine learning model that is designed to recognize traffic signals in real time, on the road. It is trained on over 8,000 images to recognize a total of nine different traffic signals ranging from detours to traffic lights. Its goal? Helping improve road safety by ensuring the user doesn't miss a single stop sign. Or other traffic signals.

Try our Demo

Reliable and Accurate

Our model performs at 84% accuracy in testing, demonstrating its high performance and ability to be a tool for drivers to quickly detect nearby road signs and improve their experience on the roads.

Free to Use

Our product is completely free to use, providing everyone assistance on the roads.

A Tool, Not a Replacement

Despite the high accuracy of our model, it is not a substitute for attentive driving. At the end of the day, our model can only help and assist. The driver must always be as alert and attentive as possible to avoid accidents.

Nine Unique Classes of Road Detection

This model is able to classify Traffic Lights, Stop Signs, Road Work Ahead Signs.

ViT

Beep Beep AI utilizes a Convolutional Neural Network model for its training and testing, using Roboflow.

Easy to Use

Simply set up the camera on your dashboard and let the model catch a variety of roadsigns or traffic lights that you yourself may miss during everyday driving.

DermDetectAI - The Future of Medical Research

DermDetectAI is a machine learning solution that quickly and accurately identifies the skin condition in an image, trained on a dataset containing over 30,000 images.

Try our Demo

Reliable and Accurate

Our model performs at 89% accuracy in testing, demonstrating its high performance and ability to be a tool for doctors to quickly identify and diagnose patients.

Free to Use Forever

Our model is completely free to use and can be used by anyone to test it out and identify a condition.

A Tool, Not a Replacement

While the accuracy of this rivals that of a doctor, please be sure to not use this over a doctor's diagnosis. A doctor's word must always be final, and this is merely a tool for confirmation.

Nine classes of disease

This model is able to classify skin conditions and diseases into 9 classes, from warts to carcinomas.

ViT

DermDetectAI utilizes a vision transformer model for its training and testing, using Huggingface's transformers library and pytorch.

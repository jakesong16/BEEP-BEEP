Making Beep Beep AI

Shown below is the process we took to make Beep Beep AI, a machine learning project that performs object classification on a variety of Road Signs.

The Training Process

First, we learned about and determined the types of signs we'd want to detect. We webscraped data from Google through SerpAPI and utilized Kaggle to gather a dataset of over 3,000 images, with 9 different classifications. Early on, we faced challenges our training process, but currently the model performs at 84% accuracy.

Release and Demos

Our product release involved first uploading the webscraped images onto Roboflow, which we used to categorize our data and train a model. Next, we used Roboflow again to directly integrate it into this website which we built using html/css/js.

Our Tech Toolbox

For this project, we used Kaggle and SerpAPI to gather data, Roboflow to label and manage the dataset, and Replit for the deployment of our website.

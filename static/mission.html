Our Mission

Our mission is to improve road and driving safety through an AI model, enabling accurate road sign identification. Committed to preventing accidents, we provide a free application that equips vehicles with real-time sign object detection, promoting informed and secure driving. Our focus on simplicity and effectiveness ensures seamless integration, contributing to a safer road environment for all.
